Located in Lujiazui, Pudong, Shanghai, it is 468 meters high and is the tallest tower in Asia and the third tallest in the world. Its unique design consists of eleven spheres of different sizes connected in series, which means "big beads and small beads falling on the jade plate". It integrates sightseeing, catering, shopping, entertainment and radio and television transmission. You can overlook the beautiful scenery on both sides of the Huangpu River from the tower. It is a landmark building of Shanghai's modernization and open spirit.
