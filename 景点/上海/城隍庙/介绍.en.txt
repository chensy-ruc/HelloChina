First built during the Yongle period of the Ming Dynasty, it is an important Taoist temple in Shanghai and is dedicated to Qin Yubo, the city god. The eaves of the palace are towering, and the incense is endless. The surrounding area is connected to Yuyuan Garden and the Old Town God's Temple shopping mall, with numerous shops and snacks, integrating religious sacrifices, folk culture and bustling markets. It is a must-visit place to appreciate the style of old Shanghai.
