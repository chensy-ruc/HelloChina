Known as "China's No. 1 Commercial Street", it stretches from the Bund in the east to Jing'an Temple in the west, with a total length of more than a kilometer. There are many century-old brands and international fashion brands, the neon lights are bright and the crowds are flowing. It not only retains the historical features of the four major companies such as Yongan and Xianshi, but also brings together modern shopping and entertainment experiences. It is a classic landmark for feeling the prosperity and commercial pulse of Shanghai.
