A famous ancient town in the Jiangnan water town, with nine long streets stretching along the river, and thousands of Ming and Qing buildings standing along the water. Fangsheng Bridge spans Caogang and is magnificent. It is the largest five-hole stone bridge in Shanghai. Known as the "Shanghai Venice", the stone-paved alleys, deep courtyards and traditional snacks complement each other. It is an ideal place to experience the meaning of small bridges and flowing water.
