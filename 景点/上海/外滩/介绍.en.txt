Located on the bank of the Huangpu River, with a total length of about 1.5 kilometers, it is an iconic landscape belt in Shanghai. On the west side stand 52 classical revival and modern buildings with different styles, known as the "Architecture Expo Group of All Nations", while on the east side are Lujiazui's modern skyscrapers across the river. This place condenses the history of the opening of the port and the prosperity of the city over the past century. At night, the lights are bright, showing the charm of the "city that never sleeps".
