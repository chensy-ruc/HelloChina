It was built in memory of Yue Fei, a famous general who fought against the Jin Dynasty in the Southern Song Dynasty. The Martyrs' Shrine in the temple enshrines the seated statue of Yue Fei. The stone statues on both sides of the tomb are solemn and solemn. Under the tomb gate are four kneeling iron statues of sycophants. The couplet "The green mountains have the honor to bury the loyal bones, and the innocent white iron casts the sycophants", which has been said for thousands of years. It is a place to pay tribute to the heroes and realize the righteousness.
