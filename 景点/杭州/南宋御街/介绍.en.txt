As the central axis and royal road of Lin'an Prefecture, the capital of the Southern Song Dynasty, it was specially used for the emperor "Simeng" to worship his ancestors. It connected the political center with the prosperous market and was full of shops. It was a veritable "CBD" at that time. Today's neighborhood has been organically updated, cleverly integrating the styles of past dynasties, and is like an "architectural history museum" that vividly reproduces the memory of the ancient capital eight hundred years ago.
