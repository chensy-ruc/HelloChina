Surrounded by mountains. The village is the birthplace of West Lake Longjing tea. The tea produced in this village ranks first among the four major brands of "Lion, Dragon, Cloud and Tiger", and is known as the "No. 1 Tea Village". The village is home to many historic sites such as the Old Longjing Imperial Tea Garden, the Eighteen Imperial Tea Trees, and the Eight Views of Longjing. Historical celebrities such as Su Shi and Qianlong have left their footprints here. It is a quiet place where natural scenery and tea culture and history complement each other.
