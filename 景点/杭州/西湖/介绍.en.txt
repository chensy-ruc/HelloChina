There are clouds and mountains on three sides, a city on one side, and the lakes and mountains complement each other. Since the Tang Dynasty, continuous dredging and construction have formed a pattern of "one mountain, two towers, three islands, three embankments, and five lakes". The "Ten Scenes of the West Lake" such as the spring dawn on the Su Causeway and the remaining snow on the broken bridge are famous all over the world. Bai Juyi, Su Shi and other literati of the past dynasties left countless poems. It was included in the World Heritage List in 2011 and is the only lake cultural heritage in China. Boating on the lake, the mountains in the distance are covered with dark blue, and the water near is like smoke. The scenery changes in four seasons, whether it is sunny or rainy.
