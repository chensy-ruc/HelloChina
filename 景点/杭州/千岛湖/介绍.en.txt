The Xin'an River Reservoir is located in Chun'an County, Hangzhou. It is an artificial lake formed by intercepting the Xin'an River. There are 1,078 green islands of different shapes in the lake, hence the name Qiandao Lake. The lake is a national first-class water body and is known as "the most beautiful water in the world". It is also known as "the middle reaches of the Thousand Islands Blue Water Painting".
