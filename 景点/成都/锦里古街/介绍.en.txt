Close to the Wuhou Temple in Chengdu, it is a place where the culture of the Three Kingdoms and the folk customs of western Sichuan blend, with a total length of about 550 meters. The green tiles are scattered and the stone slabs are winding. The streets are filled with teahouses, inns, stages and handicraft workshops. Traditional snacks such as Zhang Fei Beef and Three Cannons are especially fragrant. At night, lanterns are hung high, and shadow puppet shows and Sichuan Opera face-changing performances take turns. It is known as the "No. 1 Street in Xishu".
