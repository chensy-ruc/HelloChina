It is a cultural industrial park renovated on the site of the former state-owned Hongguang Electronic Tube Factory. Adhering to the concept of "repairing old buildings as they were before and reusing old buildings for new purposes", industrial relics such as Soviet-style red brick factory buildings, steam locomotives, chimneys and water towers have been completely preserved. With music performances, digital cultural creations and fashionable entertainment as its core business formats, the park integrates rich history and modern vitality. It is a trendy landmark for experiencing industrial aesthetics and urban memory.
