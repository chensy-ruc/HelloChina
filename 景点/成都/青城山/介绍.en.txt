Located in the southwest of Dujiangyan City, it is one of the birthplaces of Taoism and has the reputation of "Qingcheng is the quietest place in the world". The whole mountain is covered with verdant trees, peaks and mountains, thousands of steps of elixir ladders, and winding paths leading to secluded areas. Taoist temples such as Tianshi Cave, Shangqing Palace, and Jianfu Palace are built against the mountains, integrating natural landscapes and Taoist culture. They are excellent places for self-cultivation and spiritual exploration, and are also listed as world cultural heritage.
