Chengdu is the capital of Sichuan Province. It has enjoyed the reputation of "Land of Abundance" since ancient times. It has a historical heritage that the city's site has not been moved and its name has not been changed for more than 3,000 years. The sun bird gold ornaments unearthed at the Jinsha site bear witness to the glory of the ancient Shu civilization. The urban layout is centered on the Imperial City and radiates in a fan shape. Jinli, Kuanzhai Alley and other blocks retain the urban style of the Ming and Qing Dynasties. Chengdu is also the gourmet capital of the world, and Sichuan cuisine is famous for its "one dish, one style, one hundred dishes and one hundred flavors". The Giant Panda Breeding Research Base is a world-renowned institution for protecting endangered animals. Today's Chengdu is a blend of tradition and modernity: Wuhou Temple, Du Fu Thatched Cottage coexist with the Financial City and Tianfu New District, with a leisurely pace of life and a rich cultural atmosphere. It is a western central city with both historical depth and livable characteristics.
