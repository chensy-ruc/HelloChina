Located in the center of Chengdu, it was built in 1924 and was named after the meaning of the Tao Te Ching, which means "everyone is bustling, like enjoying the Tailao, like climbing the spring platform". As a century-old commercial street, it is home to time-honored brands and fashion brands, with bustling crowds and delicious food. IFS Wall Climbing Panda is adjacent to Taikoo Li, a blend of tradition and modernity. It is known as the "No. 1 Commercial Street in the Midwest" and is an excellent place to experience the prosperity and fireworks of Chengdu.
