The royal palace of 24 emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties, also known as the Forbidden City. Walking among the 720,000 square meters of red walls and yellow tiles, you can travel through six hundred years of history and feel the solemnity of the three main halls and the depth of the harem. It is not only the largest and most complete ancient wooden structure complex in the world, but also contains more than 1.86 million precious cultural relics. It can be called the top palace of Chinese civilization.
