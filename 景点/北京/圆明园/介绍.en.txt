The large-scale royal garden built during the heyday of the Qing Dynasty combines Jiangnan scenic spots with Western architectural wonders and is known as the "Garden of Ten Thousand Gardens". After the war, the ruins of stone pillars remained, now a heritage park. Walking around, you can not only think about the exquisite gardening art of the past, but also feel the dignity of the vicissitudes of history. It is a unique place to visit ancient times and contemplate the past.
