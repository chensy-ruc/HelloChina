Xiangshan Park is located in the western suburbs of Beijing. It is one of the "Three Mountains and Five Gardens" of the royal gardens of the Qing Dynasty. There are towering ancient trees in the park, and it is especially famous for its red leaves in late autumn, which make it a spectacular sight. There are also historical relics such as Xiangshan Temple and Shuangqing Villa here. The natural landscape and cultural heritage complement each other, making it an excellent place to visit and nostalgic for the past.
