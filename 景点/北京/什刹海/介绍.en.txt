Shichahai is located in Xicheng District, Beijing. It is the remnant of Jishuitan in the Yuan Dynasty and consists of three parts: Qianhai, Houhai and Xihai. Surrounded by blue waves and surrounded by weeping willows, the "Yinding Mountain View" is one of the eight scenic spots in Yanjing. Surrounded by many cultural relics and historic sites such as Prince Gong's Mansion and Soong Ching Ling's Former Residence, it combines the charm of a water town with the fireworks of the city. It is an excellent place to appreciate the charm and leisure elegance of old Beijing.
