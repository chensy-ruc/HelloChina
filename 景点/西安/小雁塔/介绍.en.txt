The dense-eaves brick pagoda in Jianfu Temple was originally fifteen stories high but now has thirteen stories. It has a beautiful and straight shape. The tower body split and rejoined after an earthquake, which is a wonder. In the past, the bells of Jianfu Temple would ring melodiously every morning, echoing in the pagoda courtyard. The "Morning Bell of Wild Goose Pagoda" became one of the eight scenic spots in Guanzhong. Today it is adjacent to the Xi'an Museum, with towering ancient trees. It is a quiet place to explore the charm of the Tang Dynasty and meditate on history.
