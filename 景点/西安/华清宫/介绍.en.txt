Located at the foot of Li Mountain in Lintong, Xi'an, it was the palace of the emperors of the Tang Dynasty and is famous for its natural hot springs. The ruins of the soup pool in the palace still exist, witnessing the grand scene of "bathing in Huaqing Pool in the cold spring". The "Xi'an Incident" that shocked China and foreign countries also happened in Wujianting. This place integrates royal gardens, bathing culture and modern historical sites. It has pleasant scenery in all seasons, especially the live performance of "Song of Everlasting Sorrow" which is the most moving.
