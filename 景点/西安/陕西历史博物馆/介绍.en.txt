China's first large-scale modern national museum. Designed by Academician Zhang Jinqiu, the architectural complex imitating the Tang Dynasty has a solemn and elegant layout of "axial symmetry, central hall, and towers at four corners." The museum has a collection of more than 1.7 million pieces (groups), among which the Shang and Zhou bronzes, pottery figurines of the past dynasties, Tang Dynasty murals and gold and silverware are the most famous. It is known as "the pearl of the ancient capital and the treasure house of China". The collection of national treasure-level cultural relics such as an agate cup inlaid with gold animal heads and a gilt silver pot with dancing horse's cup pattern can be called a condensed history of Chinese civilization.
