The core neighborhood of Millennium Huifang is paved with bluestones and lined with shops. There are hundreds of authentic halal snacks here, including beef and mutton steamed buns, meat buns, steamer cakes, etc. The aroma is overflowing. The diverse Islamic customs are intertwined with Guanzhong's urban culture, and it is crowded with people day and night. It is a must-visit place to experience Xi'an's fireworks and food traditions.
