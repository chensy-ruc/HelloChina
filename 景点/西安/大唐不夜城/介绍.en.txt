Located at the foot of the Big Wild Goose Pagoda in Xi'an, it is the Tangfeng Pedestrian Street with a length of 2,100 meters from north to south. Five themed sculpture groups are distributed in the center of the street, recreating the atmosphere of the prosperous Zhenguan era. At night, the lights are bright, the Tang Dynasty-style architecture and technology are intertwined with light and shadow, and there are also performance art performances. This place integrates sightseeing, shopping, and entertainment, making it a sleepless place to experience the charm of Tang Dynasty culture.
